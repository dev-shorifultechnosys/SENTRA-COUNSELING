Everything you need to know about getting started

Online counseling with Wasla works through our secure platform. After matching with a counselor, you can communicate via messaging, live chat, phone, or video sessions. You have unlimited messaging and can schedule live sessions at convenient times.

Wasla Counseling offers flexible pricing plans starting from $50 per session. We also offer package deals and financial assistance for students who qualify.

Yes! All Wasla counselors are certified professionals with relevant degrees and extensive experience in academic and career counseling. Each counselor has been vetted through our rigorous screening process.

Absolutely! Finding the right counselor fit is important. You can switch counselors at any time for any reason at no additional cost.

We offer comprehensive services including career planning, academic support, major discovery, scholarship applications, university admissions guidance, and more. Our counselors help with all aspects of your academic journey.

Research shows that online counseling can be just as effective as traditional in-person counseling for many conditions including anxiety, depression, and stress. The convenience and accessibility of online counseling often leads to better adherence and outcomes.

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All communication is encrypted and HIPAA compliant. Your counselor is bound by professional ethics and confidentiality requirements. We use bank-level security to protect your information.
